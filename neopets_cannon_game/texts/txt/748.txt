Climb into a cannon? Are you crazy? Flippy will be shot out of a cannon and into a giant barrel of water, and YOU get to control it all.Click the mouse button to begin, and the cannon will be pushed towards the foul line. Make sure you stop running before you cross the foul line. Click the screen again to begin the upswing of the cannon from 0 degrees to 90 degrees (straight upwards). Finally, click the mouse one last time to launch Flippy at the target.Points are rewarded for landing your character in the barrel of water. Gameplay continues if you land inside the barrel. Earn Neopoints based on accuracy.
